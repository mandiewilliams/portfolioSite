About Mandie

Wordsmith. Thesaurus addict. Novel junkie with ink stained fingers. I'm a writer in my heart of hearts.

Professionally, I do a bit more.

After graduating from the University of Colorado at Boulder, I started my professional career in the yarn group at Interweave (now F+W Media). As assistant editor on flagship magazines Interweave Knits and knit.wear, I gained a broad background in craft publishing-- long and short form editorial, managing freelancers, art direction, and planning. I made the shift from print to online editorial and social media management when I was promoted from assistant editor to the community manager for BurdaStyle.com. Since then, I've always bridged the gap between product development and marketing, editorial and sales, creativity and analytics.

I currently work for a web development school in NYC, remaining at the intersection of creativity and tech. Get in touch or scroll through my resume for more of what I've been up to. I am always looking for freelance opportunities to keep my pen moving.

I make time outside of work to play roller derby, catch as much live music as possible and immerse myself in the NYC food scene. My freelance work is an outlet for my interests: fiction, beauty, pop feminism, music, and food.

Mandie has always been my byline. When I moved to NYC, the byline came first, followed by the girl.
